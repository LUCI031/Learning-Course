Pork Cutlet

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Pork meat 300g
Pepper 1 tsp
Powder 50g
Oil 300ml

Step 1: Hit up the oil on pan.
Step 2: Season pork with pepper.
Step 3: Cover it up with powder.
Step 4: Oil it least 7 min.

Tomato Pasta

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato source 500g
Pepper 1 tsp
Noodles 50g
Oil 30ml

Step 1: Hit up the oil on pan.
Step 2: Pour up the cooked noodles.
Step 3: Pour up tomato source.
Step 4: Mix it.
